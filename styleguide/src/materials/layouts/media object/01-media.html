--- notes: | The 'media' object is a common pattern in OOCSS. It consists of a wrapper element with a class of `.media` and then two child elements, one that contains an image or video that is wrapped in either a class of `.media__left` to float left or `.media__right` to float right, and the other contains text. This used commonly in bios, or comments where you have an avatar to one side, and the comment next to it. ---
